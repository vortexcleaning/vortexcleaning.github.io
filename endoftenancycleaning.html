End of Tenancy Cleaning Auckland

Leave Your Property Spotless and Bond-Ready

Vortex Cleaning provides reliable and professional end of tenancy cleaning in Auckland. Our expert cleaners ensure your property is left spotless, helping you secure your full bond.

What do we clean?

Living Areas

Dusting, vacuuming, and wiping all surfaces including walls, skirting boards, and switches.

Kitchen

Deep cleaning of benchtops, sinks, cupboards, splashbacks, stovetops, rangehoods, and ovens.

Bathrooms

Complete sanitation of toilets, showers, baths, vanities, mirrors, and tiles, floor mopping and general dusting.

Bedrooms

Dusting, vacuuming, wardrobe wipe-down,skirting board cleaning, air freshening and cobweb removal.

Garages

Dusting all surfaces, removing cobwebs, wiping down benches, cleaning doors and door frames (excluding roller doors) and thorough vacuuming.
